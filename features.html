Powerful Features for Industrial Excellence

Discover how DIME transforms your industrial data into actionable intelligence

Comprehensive Industrial Connectivity

Connect to any industrial device, system, or protocol with our extensive library of 40+ native connectors. No gateways, no middleware, just direct connectivity.

  • OPC-UA, OPC-DA for legacy and modern systems
  • Modbus TCP/RTU for PLCs and sensors
  • Ethernet/IP for Allen-Bradley equipment
  • MTConnect for CNC machines
  • MQTT, SparkplugB for IIoT devices
  • Database connectors for SQL, PostgreSQL, InfluxDB

AI-Powered Analytics with HTM

Revolutionary Hierarchical Temporal Memory (HTM) technology provides brain-inspired continuous learning and pattern recognition for your industrial data.

  • Continuous learning without model retraining
  • Real-time anomaly detection and scoring
  • Predictive maintenance with temporal patterns
  • Self-adapting to process changes
  • Explainable AI with visual insights

Real-Time Data Monitoring

  • Performance metrics and KPI tracking
  • Automated alerting and notifications
  • Historical trend analysis

Interactive Data Explorer

Explore and analyze your industrial data with our intuitive data explorer. Find insights, track trends, and make data-driven decisions faster.

  • Browse all connected data sources
  • Interactive charting and visualization
  • Statistical analysis (min, max, avg, current)

  • Export data for further analysis
